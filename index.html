About lionel messi

football player

Lionel Messi is a football player from Argentina who plays for Inter Miami. He has won the Ballon D'Or, the annual award given to the best player in the world, 8 times, 2022 FIFA World Cup winner and an Olympic gold medal winner in 2008. He was born in 1987 in Rosario, Argentina's third-biggest city. He showed an enormous aptitude for football and was in the youth teams for Newell's Old Boys, his local team. Faced with mounting medical expenses to treat a growth hormone condition, Messi's family accepted an offer to move the 13-year-old prodigy to FC Barcelona, who would pay for his treatment. Messi has gone on to become one of the most decorated players in football history and has broken countless records for his club and his country.

carrier

Messi started his professional career at Barcelona, where he went to spend the majority of his career. He rose through the ranks of La Masia academy and made his debut for the first team in a friendly against Porto in November 2003, but was only promoted one year later, by Dutch manager Frank Rijkaard. He signed his first senior contract on his eighteenth birthday, and gradually established himself in the starting team, initially as a right winger. His teammate Ronaldinho was, at the time, one of the best players of the team but his form started to decline and eventually he left for Milan in 2008. Messi then inherited his number ten jersey and became Barça's new star player at only twenty-one years old. Under the leading of managers such as Luis Enrique and specially Pep Guardiola, Messi went on to obtain several trophies with the catalans. In July 2021, he became a free agent after his contract had expired; negotiations with Barcelona for a new deal complicated after financial issues at the Spanish club. Eventually, in August, Messi joined French side Paris Saint-Germain on a two-year deal. Following confirmation of his departure from PSG in the summer of 2023, Messi was linked with a return to former club Barcelona. However, that same summer, he joined American side Inter Miami on a two-and-a-half year contract.
